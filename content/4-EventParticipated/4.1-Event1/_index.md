# Summary Report: "Vietnam Cloud Day 2025: Ho Chi Minh City Connect Edition - Live Telecast Session"

### Event Overview

While the main event took place in Hanoi, AWS brought a seamless hybrid experience to Ho Chi Minh City. The Live Telecast session featured the plenary session live-streamed from Hanoi, including keynote speakers and breakthrough announcements shaping Vietnam's cloud future.

**Key Focus Areas:**

- Generative AI: Latest developments and practical applications
- Data Analytics: Business transformation through data-driven insights
- Migration & Modernization: Cloud transformation journey guidance

### Speakers

- **Eric Yeo** – Country General Manager, Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos & Myanmar, AWS
- **Jaime Valles** – Vice President, Commercial Sales & Business Development APJ, AWS
- **Jeff Johnson** – Managing Director ASEAN, AWS
- **Dr. Jens Lottner** – Chief Executive Officer, Techcombank
- **Ms. Trang Phung** – CEO & Co-Founder, U2U Network
- **Vu Van** – Co-founder & CEO, ELSA Corp
- **Nguyen Hoa Binh** – Chairman, Nexttech Group
- **Dieter Botha** – CEO, TymeX

#### Opening Session

#### Customer Keynote 1: Techcombank

#### Customer Keynote 2: U2U Network

#### Panel Discussion: Navigating the GenAI Revolution - Strategies for Executive Leadership

This panel discussion delved into how executive leaders can effectively steer their organizations through the rapid advancements in generative AI.

- **Moderator**: Jeff Johnson, Managing Director, ASEAN, AWS
- **Panelists**:
  - Vu Van, Co-founder & CEO, ELSA Corp
  - Nguyen Hoa Binh, Chairman, Nexttech Group
  - Dieter Botha, CEO, TymeX

**Key Discussion Points:**

- Fostering a culture of innovation within organizations
- Aligning AI initiatives with business objectives
- Managing organizational changes that accompany AI integration
- Personal journeys and insights from executive leaders on AI adoption

### Key Takeaways

#### Strategic Vision

- **Cloud-first approach**: Vietnam is rapidly adopting cloud technologies for digital transformation
- **GenAI adoption**: Executive leaders emphasized the importance of integrating generative AI into business strategies
- **Customer success stories**: Real examples from Techcombank and U2U Network demonstrated practical cloud adoption

#### Executive Insights

- **Innovation culture**: Organizations need to foster an environment that embraces AI experimentation
- **Business alignment**: AI initiatives must align with core business objectives for maximum impact
- **Change management**: Successful AI integration requires careful management of organizational transitions

#### Industry Trends

- **Hybrid cloud experiences**: AWS enables seamless connectivity across regions
- **Financial sector innovation**: Banks like Techcombank are leading cloud adoption in Vietnam
- **Web3 and emerging tech**: Companies like U2U Network are building on AWS for next-generation applications

### Applying to Work

- **Embrace cloud-first mindset**: Consider AWS services for application development and deployment
- **Explore GenAI capabilities**: Investigate how generative AI can enhance productivity and innovation
- **Learn from industry leaders**: Apply lessons from successful organizations like Techcombank and ELSA
- **Focus on business alignment**: Ensure technology initiatives support core business objectives
- **Foster innovation culture**: Create an environment that encourages experimentation with new technologies
