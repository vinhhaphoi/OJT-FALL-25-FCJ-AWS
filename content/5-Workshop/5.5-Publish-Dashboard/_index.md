#### Step 1: Arrange Visuals

1.  You can resize and move the visuals on the sheet to make them look organized.
2.  Click on the title of each visual to rename it (e.g., "Sales by Product").
3.  Add a title to the sheet at the top (e.g., "Sales Overview Dashboard").

#### Step 2: Publish Dashboard

1.  At the top right, choose **Share** > **Publish dashboard**.
2.  **Publish new dashboard as**: Enter a name, e.g., `Sales-Dashboard-v1`.
3.  Choose **Publish dashboard**.
4.  You can now share this dashboard with other users in your QuickSight account or share it via a link.

Congratulations! You have successfully created a Business Intelligence dashboard using Amazon QuickSight.
