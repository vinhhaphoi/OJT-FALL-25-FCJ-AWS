#### Clean up resources

To avoid incurring future charges, you should unsubscribe from QuickSight if you do not plan to use it further.

1.  **Manage QuickSight**:
    - Click on your user icon in the top right corner.
    - Choose **Manage QuickSight**.
2.  **Unsubscribe from QuickSight**:
    - Go to **Account settings**.
    - Choose **Delete account** (this unsubscribes you from QuickSight).
    - Type `delete` to confirm.
    - Choose **Delete account**.

_Note: If you are on the Free Trial, you will not be charged until the trial ends, but it is good practice to clean up if you are done._
