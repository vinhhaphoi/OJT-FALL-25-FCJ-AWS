#### Step 1: Create an Analysis

1.  If you are not already in the analysis view, go to **Datasets**, select your dataset, and choose **Create analysis**.
2.  You will see an empty workspace called a "Sheet".

#### Step 2: Visual 1 - Sales by Product (Donut Chart)

1.  In the **Visual types** pane (bottom left), select the **Donut chart** icon.
2.  From the **Fields list** (left), drag `Product` to the **Group/Color** well.
3.  Drag `Sales` to the **Value** well.
4.  You should see a donut chart showing sales distribution by product.

#### Step 3: Visual 2 - Sales Trend over Time (Line Chart)

1.  Choose **Add** > **Add visual** from the top menu bar.
2.  Select the **Line chart** icon.
3.  Drag `Date` to the **X axis** well.
4.  Drag `Sales` to the **Value** well.
5.  You should see a line chart showing sales over time.

#### Step 4: Visual 3 - Sales by Region (Bar Chart)

1.  Choose **Add** > **Add visual**.
2.  Select the **Vertical bar chart** icon.
3.  Drag `Region` to the **X axis** well.
4.  Drag `Sales` to the **Value** well.
5.  You can now see which region performs best.
