#### Amazon QuickSight

**Amazon QuickSight** is a cloud-scale business intelligence (BI) service that you can use to deliver easy-to-understand insights to the people who you work with, wherever they are. QuickSight connects to your data in the cloud and combines data from many different sources. In a single data dashboard, QuickSight can include AWS data, third-party data, big data, spreadsheet data, SaaS data, B2B data, and more.

#### Key Features

- **SPICE**: The Super-fast, Parallel, In-memory Calculation Engine. SPICE is engineered to rapidly perform advanced calculations and serve data.
- **Auto-Graph**: Automatically selects the best visualization for your data.
- **ML Insights**: Leverages machine learning to uncover hidden trends and outliers.
- **Embedded Analytics**: Embed dashboards into your applications.
